Our morning

Our
focaccia.

Every morning, before dawn, we light the oven and start kneading. Sourdough, good olive oil, coarse salt — the same recipe as always, made in house.

Out of the oven around eight. Warm at the counter for breakfast, on the table beside the pasta at lunch, still warm on the aperitivo board. It's the thread that holds the day together.

№ 01 — Breakfast

Morning,
done right.

By 7 the first bake is already on the counter: brioche, girelle, veneziane and fagottini, warm and straight out of the oven. The cappuccino comes while you choose. It's our way of saying good morning, every day.
